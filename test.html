About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Applied Mathematics at Aix-Marseille University, working under the supervision of Prof. Kai Schneider and Dr. Keigo Matsuda. My research focuses on particle dynamics in fully developed turbulence, utilizing tessellation, multiresolution, and machine learning methods.

Research Interests

  • Particle-Laden Turbulence
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics
  • Tessellation Methods
  • High-Performance Computing
  • Machine Learning
  • High-Speed Reacting Flows
  • Combustion
  • Molecular Dynamics
